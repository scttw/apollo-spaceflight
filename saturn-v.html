01 Saturn V — Launch Complex 39

The rocket that left the planet

Meet the Saturn V — the giant rocket that carried astronauts away from Earth. It was taller than a 36‑floor building, and the most powerful rocket ever flown.

It blasted off from a pad by the sea in Florida. The rocket was built in three stacked parts called stages. Each one burned its fuel, gave a huge push, then dropped away to make the ship lighter for the next big push toward the Moon.
